Top 5 page designs

1. Rounded Corners with Drop Shadow

The reason this is a good design is because it is very easy to make,
very eye pleasing to look at and maintains that simplistic feel that some websites
should have

2. Glowing Backgrounds

A glowy background looks nice because it introduces an element of visual intrigue that captures our attention
and enriches the overall aesthetic of the design. Its gentle,radiant quality imparts a pleasing and captivating
ambiance that draws the viewer's eye, making the entire composition more visually appealing and engaging.

3. Square Loading Screens



















A square loading screen is visually appealing because its inherent symmetry
and balanced proportions create an aesthetically pleasing and harmonious
design. This symmetry not only delights the eye but also imparts a feeling
of structure and tidiness, contributing to a sense of order and organization
that enhances the overall user experience.

4. Circle Loading Screens

The same reason as number one it just fits that simplistic or minimalistic feel
that some websites should have.

5. Masked Images

Masked image designs are highly effective when used on websites due to their
ability to introduce a creative and captivating visual component. This not only
grabs the users' attention but also elevates the content's overall appeal, rendering
it more intriguing and likely to be remembered by visitors. This captivating and
memorable quality makes masked image designs an excellent choice for enhancing the
user experience on websites.
